## Platform-wide TRUE-ARB scan (`arb_scan.py`, live snapshot) — ~zero
- Enumerated EVERY active 2-outcome order-book market via CLOB cursor (2,165 markets; 202
  multi-outcome groups). Live books for all 4,326 tokens.
- **Category A (YES+NO ask-sum < $1): 0.** Every market's ask-sum ≥ $1.00 (714 pinned at exactly
  1.00) — bot-arbed tight.
- **Category A2 (multi-outcome): the YES-sum<1 readings are NOT true arbs** (incomplete-field /
  "other" risk + thin stale asks → directional, not locked). The genuinely-locked NO-sum<N−1: **0.**
- **Category B (weather impossible-bucket; UMA-resolved-but-tradable): 0** takeable.
- Conclusion: no real locked arbitrage exists on Polymarket right now; the basic arbs are eliminated
  within ms by bots, and the apparent multi-outcome ones fail the exhaustiveness requirement.
